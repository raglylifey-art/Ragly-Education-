Before & After School Care

We provide a safe, engaging environment for school-age children. With space for up to 24 kids, our program focuses on homework help, social skills, and creative play before and after the school bell rings.

Inclusive & Neurodiverse Support

At Ragly Education Center, we believe every child deserves a curriculum tailored to their needs. We specialize in supporting children with Autism and sensory processing needs through:

  • Quiet Zones: Dedicated calm-down spaces for children who need a break from overstimulation.
  • Visual Schedules: Using clear icons and routines to help children navigate their day with confidence.
  • Sensory-Integrated Play: Activities designed to build motor skills and comfort in a way that respects each child's sensory threshold.
  • Small Group Ratios: Ensuring your child receives the individual attention they deserve.
